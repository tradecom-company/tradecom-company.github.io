About TradeCom

TradeCom is a dynamic digital platform that connects sellers, service providers, delivery agents, and customers in one unified marketplace. Whether you are a business owner showcasing your products or a customer looking for trusted services nearby — TradeCom brings everything to your fingertips.

Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. The information we collect is strictly used to improve your experience and offer secure services. Here’s what we collect:

  • User Details: Name, contact info, account type, location.
  • Payment Data: Used only to manage subscriptions and verify payments.
  • Usage Activity: Helps us understand trends and improve features.

We never share or sell your data. All sensitive info is encrypted and securely stored.

Terms & Conditions

  1. Users must provide accurate and truthful registration details.
  2. Business and delivery agent accounts are subject to subscription terms.

  4. All users are responsible for the content they upload (e.g., product images, descriptions).
  5. TradeCom reserves the right to update policies to ensure safety and quality of service.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • Q: Is TradeCom free?
    A: Normal user accounts are free. Businesses and delivery agents subscribe to use premium features.
  • Q: How can I advertise my service?
    A: Log into your dashboard and use the advertisement feature to upload photos/videos with descriptions.
  • Q: How do I pay for a subscription?
    A: Use mobile money (e.g., M-Pesa) through our secure in-app payment gateway.
  • Q: Can I edit or remove a product later?
    A: Yes! Business owners can manage products directly from their dashboard.
